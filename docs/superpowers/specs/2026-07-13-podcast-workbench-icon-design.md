# Podcast Workbench Icon Design

## Goal

Create the first icon in a personal macOS tool collection. The icon should feel
friendly and collectible while remaining legible as a small Finder/Desktop icon.

## Collection System

The collection is named "Tool Pantry" (工具杂货铺). Different tools may use
fruit, coffee, cat, or dog characters. Cohesion comes from shared visual rules
rather than forcing every tool into one subject family:

- Square macOS-style tile with a generous rounded corner.
- Soft, pale background with a subtle lower horizon band.
- Simple character silhouette with one functional prop.
- Small coral status dot at the upper right.
- Warm, clean illustration with restrained facial detail.
- No words, letters, or tiny decorative elements inside the icon.

Future examples include a coffee character for a morning/daily-report tool and
fruit characters for writing, notes, or research tools.

## Podcast Workbench Icon

The podcast workbench uses the "Orange Cat Monitor" character:

- A centered orange cat head wearing teal over-ear headphones.
- The headphones are the strongest functional cue and remain visible at 32 px.
- The cat expression is calm and cheerful, cute without looking juvenile.
- Background is warm pale cream with a light lower band.
- A coral status dot sits at the upper right.
- Shapes are bold, softly dimensional, and free of outlines that become noisy at
  small sizes.

The icon must not include microphones, waveform text, app names, watermarks, or
personal information.

## Deliverables

- High-resolution square PNG master at 1024 x 1024.
- macOS `.icns` icon derived from the approved master when local tools permit.
- A 128 x 128 preview used to verify small-size readability.

The final files live under `assets/tool-pantry/` and use descriptive filenames.

## Acceptance Checks

- The subject reads as an orange cat with headphones at 128 px and 32 px.
- The silhouette does not blend into the background.
- No text or personal data appears in the image or filename.
- The icon looks coherent against the user's cool, ocean-toned desktop.
- The asset can be reused as the visual baseline for later Tool Pantry icons.
